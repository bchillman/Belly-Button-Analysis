Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This project is inspecting the different types of bacteria in various peoples' belly buttons in order to identify potential bacteria to create a new type of meatless protein. By changing the Test subject ID no. you can look at the bacteria of various belly buttons, which are shown in two different visualizations. The washing frequency of the subject is also shown to demonstrate which bacteria might be present depending on how frequently the subject washes their belly button.
